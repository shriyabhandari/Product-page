Jeep

After helping to preserve democracy during WWII, Jeep is now helping to bring its go-anywhere capability to a mall parking lot near you. The legendary Jeep Wrangler exemplifies the company's off-road pedigree, and we think it's one of the best mid-size SUVs available today. Meanwhile, the Jeep Cherokee and the Jeep Grand Cherokee are popular nameplates that boast more accessible attributes. The Gladiator merges the Wrangler's off-roading skills with the utility of a pickup. All Jeep models offer trail-ready equipment that encourages exploration. The Jeep Grand Cherokee 4xe is a luxurious off-road-capable rig, just don't expect the typical hybrid fuel savings unless you treat it like an EV.
